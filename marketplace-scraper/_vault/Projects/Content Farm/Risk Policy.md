# Risk Policy

## Core Rule

Automation should accelerate research and packaging. It should not create spam, fake engagement, impersonation, or unsupported claims.

## Platform Risks

Auto-posting is safer when:

- content is original/synthesized
- rate is reasonable
- accounts have real activity
- there is human approval
- links are not spammed
- platform APIs or trusted schedulers are used

Auto-posting is risky when:

- many accounts post similar content
- replies/DMs are automated
- content is copied from sources
- posts are misleading
- engagement is manipulated
- regulated claims are generated without review

## Content Risks

High-risk topics:

- medical advice
- investment advice
- legal advice
- credit/loan promises
- gambling/betting
- crypto trading
- identity/security fearmongering

For these, use only educational framing and disclaimers, or avoid entirely.

## Source Ethics

- Do not plagiarize.
- Link or cite original sources internally.
- Transform ideas into original commentary.
- Do not expose private/personal data.
- Do not scrape behind logins or paywalls unless you have permission.

## Approval Required

Every draft should be checked for:

- factual accuracy
- source support
- platform policy risk
- affiliate disclosure
- exaggerated claims
- tone/brand fit
