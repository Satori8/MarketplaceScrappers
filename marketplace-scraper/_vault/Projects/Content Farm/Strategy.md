# Strategy

## Thesis

The opportunity is not "AI writes posts automatically."

The opportunity is:

> Use automation to find early, under-packaged, high-intent conversations in valuable domains, then turn them into useful content faster than manual creators can.

## Money Domains

Ranked for first experiments:

1. AI tools and B2B SaaS
2. Cybersecurity, VPN, privacy, identity protection
3. Personal finance tools, budgeting, banking apps
4. Business operations: HR, accounting, CRM, sales tools
5. E-commerce seller tools
6. Health/wellness apps and telehealth
7. Legal/insurance lead gen
8. Crypto/iGaming/betting

## Practical Recommendation

Start with **AI tools / B2B SaaS**.

Reasons:

- Strong affiliate and sponsorship potential.
- Less regulated than finance, legal, health, betting.
- Fits existing skills.
- Easy to source from Reddit, X, Product Hunt, Hacker News, newsletters, RSS.
- Good match for LinkedIn, X, Telegram, YouTube Shorts.

Avoid as first domain:

- medical advice
- investment advice
- legal claims
- casino/betting
- crypto trading signals

These can make money, but they create compliance, account, and trust problems before the system is proven.

## Content Positioning

Do not copy foreign posts.

Transform source material into:

- explainers
- trend briefings
- tool comparisons
- problem/solution posts
- "what people are complaining about" reports
- buyer-intent content
- weekly digests

## Monetization Paths

### Fastest

- affiliate links
- sponsored posts
- paid newsletter
- service funnel for automation/scraping/AI setup

### Stronger Long-Term

- niche newsletter
- SEO site
- Telegram/community
- lead generation
- own micro-products/templates
- B2B reports

## Editorial Edge

Most content farms fail because output is generic.

The edge should be:

- early signal detection
- useful synthesis
- specific examples
- local-language adaptation
- clear buyer intent
- strong formatting per platform
