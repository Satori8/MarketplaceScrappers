# Domain Map

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Money intent | Does the audience buy tools/services? |
| Content supply | Are there enough foreign sources? |
| Risk | Legal/platform/medical/financial risk. |
| Personal fit | Does it match existing skills? |
| Automation fit | Can sources be parsed reliably? |
| Monetization path | Affiliate, ads, services, leads, products. |

## Candidate Domains

| Domain | Money | Risk | Fit | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| AI tools / B2B SaaS | High | Medium | High | Best first test. |
| Cybersecurity / privacy / VPN | High | Medium | Medium | Good affiliate fit. Avoid fearmongering. |
| Personal finance apps | High | High | Medium | Strong money, regulated claims risk. |
| Business operations / HR / CRM | High | Low-Medium | High | Good for B2B service funnel. |
| E-commerce seller tools | Medium-High | Low-Medium | High | Strong fit with parser/ECOBUD work. |
| Health/wellness apps | High | High | Medium | Avoid medical claims. |
| Legal / insurance | Very High | Very High | Low-Medium | Lead gen possible, compliance hard. |
| Crypto / iGaming / betting | Very High | Very High | Medium | Dangerous first niche. Account risk. |

## Recommended Order

1. AI tools / B2B SaaS
2. E-commerce seller tools
3. Business operations automation
4. Cybersecurity/privacy
5. Finance-lite

## Finance-Lite Definition

Allowed:

- budgeting tools
- expense tracking
- freelance invoicing
- tax organization tools
- business banking comparisons with disclaimers

Avoid:

- investment advice
- trading signals
- credit repair promises
- guaranteed income claims
