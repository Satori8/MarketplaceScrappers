# ECOBUD

## Objective

Build a repeatable e-commerce content and data workflow for Ukrainian marketplace listings.

Focus:

- product descriptions
- short descriptions
- marketplace-specific SEO
- keyword research
- characteristic normalization
- competitive/OSINT research where useful
