# Decisions

## Channel

- The channel is a psychology education channel, not therapy and not generic self-help.
- The audience wants recognition and explanation, not advice-heavy content.
- Narrator is a translator, not a guru.

## Visual Identity

- The recurring character wears a yellow knit sweater.
- The character is featureless and projection-friendly.
- Emotional expressions must be readable at thumbnail size.
- Backgrounds provide atmosphere and context.

## Production

- Still frames plus Ken Burns movement are the default format.
- Every generated frame should be safe for crop, pan, and zoom.
- Timing data should eventually drive visual decisions, not just subtitles.
